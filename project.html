social engineering

is an attempt to manage social changes in the future and the development and behavior of a society. Social engineering is also the use of deception to manipulate into divulging confidential or personal information. Common types include: phishing, pretexting, and baiting

Who started this?

Jacques Marken is the person who developed the term social engineering. He started using the term social engineering in the 1890. He uses this to improve the social world.
